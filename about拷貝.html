COAST Joint Lab
Exploring Earth Science in Nearshore Environments

The coastal zone, extending from the coastal land area to the nearshore offshore region, commonly referred to as the Coastal White Ribbon, is a region of paramount importance in both earth science and ecological value. It encompasses crucial issues such as active tectonics, coastal erosion, and coastal landslides. However, due to the challenges associated with surveying shallow water areas, it has become a research domain plagued by data gaps. Overcoming this obstacle is a challenge we are determined to tackle head-on.
