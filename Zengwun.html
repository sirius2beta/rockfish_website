Zengwen river

The Zengwen River estuary holds significant geological importance, showcasing typical estuarine landform features. The active sedimentation processes cause large amounts of silt and sand carried by the river to deposit at the estuary, forming extensive alluvial plains and sandbars, which shape the unique estuarine landscape and impact the local ecological environment. Additionally, the strata in this area reveal historical changes in sea level, climate variations, and tectonic movements, providing valuable research material for geologists. This makes the Zengwen River estuary a crucial site for studying estuarine dynamics, sedimentation processes, and geological history. Therefore, the Zengwen River estuary is valuable not only ecologically but also for geological research and education.

About the mission
